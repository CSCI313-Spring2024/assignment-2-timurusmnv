About Me

Hello! I'm Timur Usmanov, a 20-year-old student originally from Tashkent, Uzbekistan. Currently pursuing Computer Science with an intent to transition into Cybersecurity, I have a fervent passion for Computer Sciences and Information Technologies. Since my move to the United States in 2021, I've accumulated 2.5 years of experience working with the IT Department at Dickinson State University. Recently, I've been fortunate to join the IT Department at North Dakota State University, where I aim to leverage my leadership and management skills. While coding may not be my forte, I excel in team leadership and motivation, ensuring productivity and cohesion in any team I am a part of.
